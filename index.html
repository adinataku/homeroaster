Currently most batch goes in as 150g into the roaster. Output depends on the roast level.

You will receive approximately somewhere between 100g - 120g in a 4oz or 6 oz bag.

The coffee bag will have one way valve and it is resealable.

If you are not going to use the beans right away, you can tape the valve and store it in the freezer.

Available beans depend on seasons. It will take a day to get the roast ready and we cup the result to ensure

quality. We will text you a message when it is available for mail/delivery/pickup.

If you see a bean you like to try, let us know what roast level you want.

We mainly source green beans from Sweet Maria. Check out their website
