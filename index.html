So his hope is that the donation can help him purchase larger capacity roaster so he can do it faster.

Here's the lowdown:

  • The bean: Single Origin Sulawesi Honey Enrekang Angin Angin, sourced from Sweet Maria.
  • The flavor: You choose! Light, medium, or dark roast, depending on what you're craving.
  • The size: Each batch is made with 150g of green beans, which nets you a 100-120g 6oz bag of freshly roasted goodness.
  • The bag: Resealable with a one-way valve for freshness. If you need to stash it long-term, tape the valve and pop it in the freezer.
  • The wait: It takes a day to get it roasted and taste-tested for quality. You'll get a text when it's ready for mail, delivery, or pickup.
